Founded by Jay and Honour, Sanctum was built on the belief that your tattoo experience should feel as thoughtful and personal as the art you leave with.

Located on Victoria Embankment in West Bridgford, our studio is meticulously clean, welcoming, and designed to put you at ease — whether it's your first tattoo or your fifteenth. We work on custom designs, cover-ups, and professional piercings, all tailored entirely to you.

Healing Guide

Tattoo
aftercare

The work doesn't stop when you leave the studio. Proper care protects your investment.

01
Keep covered

Leave the wrap on for 2–4 hours after your session, or as advised by your artist. Resist the urge to peek early.

02
Wash gently

Lukewarm water, fragrance-free soap. Pat dry with clean paper towel — never rub. Avoid soaking.

03
Moisturise daily

Thin layer of unscented lotion 2–3× per day. Avoid petroleum-based products like Vaseline.

04
Protect from sun

No direct sunlight or tanning beds for 4 weeks. Once healed, always apply SPF 50+ over the tattoo.

05
No soaking

Avoid pools, hot tubs, and long baths for 2–3 weeks. Showers are fine — just don't soak.

06
Don't pick

Peeling and itching is normal. Never pick at flaking skin — let it shed on its own to preserve the detail.
