Founded by Jay and Honour, Sanctum was built on one belief: getting tattooed should feel as thoughtful and personal as the art you leave with.

Healing Guide

Tattoo
aftercare

The work doesn't end when you leave the studio. Proper care protects your investment and keeps the detail sharp for years.

Ask Us Anything →
01
Keep covered

Leave the wrap on for 2–4 hours after your session, or as your artist advises. Don't peek early.

02
Wash gently

Lukewarm water, fragrance-free soap. Pat dry with clean paper towel — never rub.

03
Moisturise daily

Thin layer of unscented lotion 2–3× per day. Avoid petroleum-based products like Vaseline.

04
Block the sun

No direct sunlight or tanning beds for 4 weeks. Once healed, always use SPF 50+.

05
No soaking

Avoid pools, hot tubs and long baths for 2–3 weeks. Showers are fine — just don't soak.

06
Don't pick

Peeling and itching is normal. Let it shed naturally to preserve the detail in your tattoo.
